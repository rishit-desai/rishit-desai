## Overview

This project involved developing a tool to streamline the management of demands within Mondelez International. It provides a centralized platform for logging, tracking, and governing demands throughout their lifecycle, from initial request to final deployment.

## Key Features

-   **Demand Logging**:  Users can log new demands with detailed descriptions and relevant information.
-   **Workflow Management**:  The tool facilitates the management of demand workflows, including approvals, assignments, and status updates.
-   **Governance**:  Implements governance processes to ensure compliance and accountability.
-   **Reporting**:  Generates reports and dashboards to provide insights into demand status and progress.

## Technologies Used

-   Python
-   QuickBase
-   GitHub Actions

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was integrating with existing systems and customizing QuickBase to meet the specific needs of the demand management process. I learned a lot about workflow automation and building enterprise-level tools.

## Outcome

The tool has improved the efficiency and transparency of the demand management process, reducing delays and improving decision-making.
