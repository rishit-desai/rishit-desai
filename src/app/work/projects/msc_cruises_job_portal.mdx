## Overview

This project involved developing a job portal for MSC Cruises, facilitating the recruitment process by providing a platform for job seekers to find and apply for positions.

## Key Features

-   **Job Listings**:  Displays available job openings.
-   **Application Management**:  Allows users to submit job applications.
-   **Search and Filtering**:  Enables users to search and filter jobs based on criteria.

## Technologies Used

-   Angular
-   Node.js
-   MySQL
-   Sequelize.js

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a scalable and user-friendly job portal with efficient search functionality was a key challenge. I gained experience in developing full-stack web applications with Angular and Node.js.

## Outcome

The job portal provides a platform for MSC Cruises to streamline their recruitment process and connect with potential employees.
